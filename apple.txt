Apple facts
Apples are round and can be red, green, or yellow.
They grow on trees and are popular in pies.
An apple a day keeps the doctor away.
Some apples are sweet, others are tart.
Some apples float in water because they are 25% air.
The crabapple is the only apple native to North America.
Apple trees can live for more than 100 years.
China produces the most apples in the world.
The largest apple ever picked weighed over 4 pounds.
